## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
When you use the Kolbo Desktop app, we may collect:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, password (stored securely)
- **Authentication Data**: Login tokens, session information
- **Profile Information**: Preferences, settings, language selection

### Desktop Application Data
The Kolbo Desktop app collects desktop-specific information:

- **Local Storage**: App settings, cache, user preferences stored on your device

- **System Information**: OS version, app version, device type
- **Error Logs**: Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability
- **Update Information**: Checks for new versions via GitHub

### User-Generated Content
- **AI Inputs**: Text prompts, instructions, and queries you provide
- **AI Outputs**: Images, videos, audio, and other content generated through the app
- **Project Data**: Organization of your content, folders, categories
- **Training Lab Data**: Custom model training data (if you use this feature)

- **IP Address**: When connecting to our servers
- **Device Information**: Hardware model, operating system
- **Performance Metrics**: App startup time, memory usage, errors

## 4. Desktop App Specific Privacy

### Local Storage
- The app stores settings, cache, and preferences locally on your device
- Location: `%APPDATA%/kolbo-desktop` (Windows) or `~/Library/Application Support/kolbo-desktop` (macOS)
- You can clear local cache from Settings
- Uninstalling removes local data

### Auto-Launch on Startup
- If enabled, the app starts when your computer boots
- You can disable this in Settings at any time
- This setting is stored locally on your device

### Update Checks
- The app periodically checks GitHub for new versions
- No personal information is sent during update checks
- Only app version and platform information

### System Tray Integration
- The app runs in your system tray when minimized
- No additional data collection occurs while in tray
